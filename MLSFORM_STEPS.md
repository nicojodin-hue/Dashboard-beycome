# MLS Form Steps Structure

## Step 1: General, Legal & Tax
- Your Full Name
- Legal Owner Name
- Signing Authority dropdown
- Owner Occupying Property (Yes/No)
- Did the Owner dropdown (< 1 year, > 1 year, doesn't own yet)
- Parcel ID/Folio #
- Additional Parcel (Yes/No)
- Year Built
- New Construction (Yes/No)
- Property Condition dropdown
- Property Style dropdown
- Tax Amount
- Tax Year
- Occupancy Information (checkboxes: Owner/Tenant/Vacant)
- Flood Zone Code
- Lot Size Acres
- Lot Description (multi-select)
- Proof of Identity/Ownership upload
- MLS Selection dropdown

## Step 2: Financial
- List Price
- Original List Price
- Possession Date
- Tax Info
- HOA Fees
- Special Assessment
- Utilities

## Step 3: Media
- Photos Upload (up to 25)
- Virtual Tour URL
- Video URL
- Floor Plan Upload
- Document Uploads

## Step 4: Interior
- Total Rooms
- Flooring Types
- Appliances Included
- Interior Features

## Step 5: Room Details
- Living Room Details
- Dining Room Details
- Kitchen Details
- Bedroom Details
- Bathroom Details

## Step 6: Exterior
- Exterior Features
- Parking Details
- Pool/Spa
- View Description
- Architectural Style

## Step 7: Community & Restriction
- HOA Information
- Community Features
- Restrictions
- Zoning

## Step 8: Agreement
- Listing Agreement Review
- Electronic Signature
- Terms Acceptance

## Step 9: Review & Submit
- Summary of all entered information
- Final review before MLS submission
