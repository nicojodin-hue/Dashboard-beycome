Our Services

Everything title, under one roof

We handle every step — from title search to final policy delivery — with licensed professionals in Florida and Texas.

01

Title Search & Examination

A thorough search of public records to verify ownership history and identify outstanding liens, judgments, or encumbrances that could affect the transfer of title.

02

Title Insurance

Issued through Old Republic National Title — one of America's most financially secure underwriters. Protects owners and lenders against defects discovered after closing.

03

Closing & Settlement

We prepare all closing documents, manage the escrow account, coordinate with all parties, and disburse funds and record the deed upon completion.

04

Escrow & Fund Management

Funds held in FDIC-insured, segregated escrow accounts with daily reconciliations. We disburse only when all closing conditions are fully met.

05

Title Clearance & Curative

If our search uncovers an issue — an old lien, recording error, or gap in the chain — our team resolves it before closing so your deal doesn't fall apart.

06

Post-Closing & Policy Delivery

After closing we record the deed with the county, issue the final title insurance policy, and maintain a full archive of all transaction documents.

FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before opening your title order.

What is title insurance and do I need it?
Title insurance protects you against financial loss from defects in a property's title — things like unknown liens, recording errors, undisclosed heirs, or forged documents. Unlike regular insurance, it covers issues from the past rather than future events. It's a one-time premium paid at closing, and it protects you for as long as you own the property.
How does Beycome Title work with my Beycome listing?
Once you have an accepted offer on your Beycome listing, you can open a title order directly — no separate intake process. We coordinate with all parties and keep you updated throughout the closing timeline.
How do you keep your fees so low?
We operate with a lean, tech-enabled team that handles more volume with less overhead. We don't pay referral kickbacks to real estate agents — those savings go directly to you in the form of lower fees.
Which states do you operate in?
We are currently licensed and operating in Florida and Texas. We're actively expanding. If your property is in another state, contact us and we may be able to refer you to a trusted partner.
Who is Old Republic and why does it matter?
Old Republic National Title Insurance Company is one of the largest underwriters in the U.S., with over 100 years of operation and $29 billion in assets. As an authorized agent, Beycome Title issues policies backed by that same financial strength — the same protection you'd get from any major title company, at a fraction of the price.
How long does the closing process take?
Title searches are typically completed within 48 hours of opening your order. Most residential closings are scheduled 2–4 weeks after going under contract. We flag any title issues early so they don't delay your closing.
Can I close remotely / digitally?
Yes. We support Remote Online Notarization (RON) in Florida, allowing you to close from anywhere via secure video. In Texas, we coordinate with local notaries and mobile signing agents. Just let us know your preference when you open your order.
